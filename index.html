About Me

Hi! I'm Wally, a passionate web developer with a keen eye for accessible and inclusive design. As a person with a disability (PWD), I understand firsthand the importance of building digital experiences that are usable by everyone, regardless of their abilities.

With three years of experience in multimedia, four years in mobile development, and eight years in front-end development (along with some knowledge of back-end development), I specialize in creating websites and applications that are not only visually appealing but also fully accessible and optimized for performance. I work with technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, using frameworks such as jQuery and Bootstrap. In mobile development, I have experience with Apache Cordova, jQuery Mobile, and Ionic.

I am passionate about creating efficient solutions, improving user experiences, and ensuring that every project meets the highest standards of usability and compliance (such as WCAG and ARIA).

My interest in new technology drives me to continuously learn and refine my skills. I strongly believe that the web should be accessible to everyone, and I am committed to advocating for digital inclusivity through my work—whether it's developing accessible web applications, optimizing UI/UX for assistive technologies, or consulting on accessibility improvements.

I'm currently working as a Frontend Developer for a company that focuses on the latest web trends and technologies, but I'm open to any part-time opportunities related to my skills.

Feel free to reach out—let's connect and make the web a more inclusive space!
